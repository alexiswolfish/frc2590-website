Inspiring Students to pursue Math, Science, and Engineering.

The members of team Nemesis innovate and problem solve in technical fieldswith assistance from engineering and business professionals to construct a competitive robot during an intensive 6-week build season. The team is run like a company, with the finance, marketing and web sub-teams working to raise and manage team funds.

The cost of parts, transportation for the robot, and transportation for the team is very expensive. The team works in many ways to raise money to cover these costs such as bagging at local supermarkets, running Robotics Discovery Days, and holding fundraisers at local restaurants. This year, Team Nemesis has won a regional and a district event, qualifying the team to compete in the World Championships in St. Louis. This opportunity comes at a cost; and your support ensures each student can afford to travel to and witness the culmination of their efforts.

FRC Team 2590, Nemesis, is an award winning FIRST Robotics team based out of Robbinsville High School in New Jersey.

Founded in 2008, the students in Nemesis routinely solve challenges in business, computer science, engineering, and math.
